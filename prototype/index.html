Phishing Simulation

Review the message below and choose the safest action.

From: CDU IT Support <security-alert@cdu-login-check.com>
Subject: Urgent: Your student account will be suspended today

Dear Student,

Your university account has failed a security verification. To avoid suspension within 2 hours, click the link below and confirm your password immediately.

https://cdu-login-check.com/verify-now

Regards,
IT Support Team

Choose your response

Immediate Feedback

The platform explains why a learner response was safe or risky.

Safe Response

The safest action is to avoid clicking the link and verify the message through official university channels. The sender domain is suspicious, the message creates urgency, and it asks for password confirmation.

Warning Signs

  • Urgent pressure: “within 2 hours”
  • Suspicious domain: cdu-login-check.com
  • Requests password confirmation
  • Threatens account suspension
